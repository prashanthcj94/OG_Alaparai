OUR STORY

How It All Started

OG Alaparai was born from the streets of Poonamallee, Chennai. We looked around and realized that everyone is stuck in the daily grind and needs more reasons to smile. We gathered a bunch of creatives, filmmakers, and comedians to build a platform for raw, organic, and relatable Tamil humor.

We don't believe in corporate structures or complex hierarchies. We believe in raw energy, creative freedom, and testing boundaries to make people laugh out loud.
